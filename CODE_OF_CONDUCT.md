# Code of Conduct

Welcome to DSAverse - a collaborative space for sharing and exploring solutions in algorithms and data structures.
We’re committed to fostering a positive, inclusive environment where every contributor’s input is valued and everyone can grow together.


## Introduction
This document sets out the behaviors expected of everyone who interacts on DSAverse, as well as the principles that guide community engagement.


## Our Standards
Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on the project and not individuals
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behaviors include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Public or private insults or attacks
- Trolling, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Disruptive behavior that interferes with project work or community discussions


## Role of Maintainers
Maintainers are here to:

- Set, explain, and uphold these standards
- Respond swiftly and fairly to any breaches
- Ensure communication remains constructive and safe

Maintainers may edit or remove content, or limit access if contributions violate these guidelines.


## Coverage
- Applies to every space related to the project - GitHub repos, issues, PRs, chats.
- Applies whenever someone represents DSAverse or its community.


## Reporting Concerns
- If you see behavior contrary to these standards, notify maintainers privately or use designated report channels.
- All reports are handled confidentially and with impartiality.

## Summary

By participating in DSAverse, you agree to uphold these principles and help us create a positive, inclusive environment for all.
